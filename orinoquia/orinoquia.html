Orinoquia Collection

Inspired by the Orinoco River, which flows through the primeval jungles of Venezuela. It is where the strength of nature, which shelters us and gives us our origin, is concentrated. This brand offers jewelry that highlights the elements of nature and, as a tribute to it, uses gold to elevate each piece to its highest expression of elegance. The final result is necklaces, earrings, bracelets, and rings with various shades, veins, openings, and curves that allow light and skin to shine through, showcasing both the jewelry and the wearer—like an embrace of nature.
