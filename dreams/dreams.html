Where Dreams Come to Life

"Where dreams come to life" serves as my artistic haven, a realm where the ethereal shapes and precious gems that dance through my dreams find tangible existence upon waking. This collection unfolds as a natural progression from the enchanting Orinoquia series, seamlessly blending organic curves, modern lines and delicate moth-inspired form adornes with exquisite quartz and emerals. The culmination of this creative journey materializes in a series of necklaces, bracelets, and earrings, each a symphony of diverese tones, intricate veins, alluring opening, and graceful curves.
